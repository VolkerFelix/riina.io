Data rights

Account and data deletion

You can ask Riina to delete your account and the personal data associated with it. Use the form below to open a pre-filled email to our privacy inbox. We use the address on file to verify your identity before processing the request.

Before you continue

Deleting your account is permanent for the account record and, where applicable, removes or anonymizes associated workout, profile, social, and analytics data as described in our privacy practices. Some information may need to be retained where the law requires it.

Submit a deletion request

Send your request to privacy@riina.io. Filling out the form and choosing “Send request email” opens your mail app with a message you can review and send.

Without automatic email

Copy the template below, replace the bracketed parts, and send it from your usual email to privacy@riina.io.

Subject: Account and data deletion request — Riina

I request deletion of my Riina account and associated personal data.

Registered email: [your email]
Username or user ID (if known): [optional]
Country/region: [optional]

I confirm that I wish to proceed with account deletion and understand it may be irreversible.

Additional notes:
[optional]

What happens next

  • We may reply from the same address to confirm details or verify that the request comes from you.
  • Processing time depends on workload and applicable law; we will acknowledge receipt when we can.
  • For how data is used today, see the Privacy Statement.
